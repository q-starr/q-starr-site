Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: Nothing leaves your device. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data.

Starr AI reads nothing from you except what you type or speak in the moment (processed entirely on your device).

Sun Light only reads sunlight exposure data from Apple HealthKit (already recorded by your Apple Watch).

What data we access

  • Starr AI: Uses on-device AI models. No data is sent anywhere.
  • Sun Light: Reads Time in Daylight from Apple HealthKit and uses your device's location only to calculate local sunrise/sunset times (all processed on your device).

Where your data stays

Everything stays on your iPhone and Apple Watch only.

There are no servers, no cloud, no analytics, and no third-party services. Your conversations (Starr AI) and sunlight progress (Sun Light) never leave your devices.

Data sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to anyone. Ever.

Between your devices

Both apps use Apple's secure, encrypted systems to communicate between your iPhone and Apple Watch. Nothing goes through external servers.

Children's privacy

The apps do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents can manage Sun Light's "Child" profile for outdoor time tracking.

Deleting your data

You can revoke access or delete the apps at any time. All local data is removed when you delete the app.

Questions?
