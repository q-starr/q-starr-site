Privacy Policy

Q Starr LLC — Last updated: April 2026

The short version: Nothing leaves your device. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data.

Starr AI is a private AI assistant that runs entirely on your iPhone. All conversations are processed using on-device models — nothing is sent to any server.

Sun Light tracks your natural sunlight exposure on Apple Watch. It accesses your Time in Daylight data from Apple HealthKit and uses your device location to calculate local sunrise and sunset times. All of this stays on your device.

What data we access

  • Starr AI: Processes your conversations using on-device AI models only
  • Sun Light: Reads Time in Daylight from Apple HealthKit
  • Sun Light: Uses device location to calculate sunrise/sunset times
  • All processing happens locally on your iPhone or Apple Watch

Where your data stays

Everything stays on your iPhone and Apple Watch. There are no servers, no cloud, no analytics, and no third-party services. Your data never leaves your device.

Data sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal data to anyone. Period.

Between your devices

Communication between your iPhone and Apple Watch uses Apple's built-in encrypted systems. Nothing is routed through external servers.

Children's privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents and guardians can manage a child profile within Sun Light.

Deleting your data

You can delete either app at any time. All local data is automatically removed when the app is deleted.
