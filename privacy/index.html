Sun Light — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

The short version: Sun Light reads your sunlight exposure data from Apple HealthKit to show you how much time you spend outdoors each day. Your data stays on your devices. We don't collect it, store it on servers, or share it with anyone.

What Data We Access

Sun Light reads a single data type from Apple HealthKit:

  • Time in Daylight — the duration of outdoor sunlight exposure recorded passively by your Apple Watch sensors. This is the only HealthKit data type the app accesses.

The app also uses your device's location (via CoreLocation) solely to calculate local sunrise and sunset times for your area. Location data is processed on-device and is not transmitted or stored.

How We Use Your Data

Your sunlight exposure data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your daily progress toward your sunlight goal
  • Show your weekly history, streaks, and time-of-day breakdown
  • Power the progress ring on your Apple Watch and home screen widgets
  • Send you optional morning sunlight reminders

Where Your Data Lives

All data remains on your personal devices:

  • HealthKit data stays in Apple's Health database on your iPhone and Apple Watch
  • App settings (daily goal, streak count) are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults
  • Session history is stored locally using SwiftData on your Apple Watch
  • Widget data is shared between the app and its widgets using a local App Group container on the same device

Sun Light has no server, no cloud storage, no backend, and no analytics service. Nothing leaves your devices.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit your health data or personal information to any third party. Period.

Data Between Your Devices

Sun Light uses Apple's WatchConnectivity framework to sync your daily goal setting between your iPhone and Apple Watch. This communication happens directly between your paired devices over Apple's encrypted channel. No data passes through external servers.

Third-Party Services

Sun Light does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not contain ads.

Children's Privacy

Sun Light includes a "Child" profile option for parents who want to track their children's outdoor time for vision health. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. A parent or guardian should set up and manage the app on their child's device.

Data Deletion

You can revoke Sun Light's access to HealthKit data at any time through Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > Sun Light on your iPhone. Deleting the app removes all locally stored settings and session data. HealthKit data is managed by Apple's Health app and is not affected by deleting Sun Light.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version here with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
